Predicting the price of cars

For this project, we got to pick our own topic we wanted to find a dataset on and see if we could create a linear model of our data. My group chose a dataset of cars. It provided us with a number of variables about the car like mileage, year, and condition and we were left to create a model that fit the data. We created a model and used it to predict the price of sally from the movie cars. We found out she was a 2006 Porsche 911 Cararra. At the time they were selling on the market for about $44,000 and our model predicted Sally to be worth just over $42,000 This project helped me become a better coder as we had to do a lot of data manipulation and model cretion in R studio.
